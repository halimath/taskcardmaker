What's new?

  • Send tasks PDF via email
    If you do your planing on a computer without a printer and want to print your tasks later you can now send them directly to your email.
  • Alter card and font size
    Now you can change the dimension of the printed cards as well as the font size.
  • Add a checkbox to every card
    By default a small checkbox is added to every taskcard so you can mark which tasks have been done. You can decide not to print checkboxes to your cards as well.
  • Add line breaks to tasks and stories.
    You can add line breaks and empty lines to task and story descriptions.
